Oyabun - Boss

The oyabun is the leader of a yakuza organization. He is the highest-ranking member and holds absolute authority over all other members. The oyabun is responsible for setting the overall direction of the organization, making major decisions, and ensuring that all members follow the rules and regulations set by him. He also serves as a mentor to younger members, providing guidance and advice on how to conduct themselves in order to maintain the honor and reputation of the organization.

Wakagashira - 2nd

The Wakagashira is the second-in-command of a yakuza organization. They are responsible for managing the day-to-day operations of the organization, including overseeing finances, recruiting new members, and mediating disputes between members. They also serve as a liaison between the organization and other yakuza groups. The Wakagashira is expected to be loyal to the Oyabun (head of the organization) and carry out their orders without question.
